ABOUT ME

Hi! I’m a graphic design student who loves bringing ideas to life through thoughtful, creative design. When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me traveling somewhere new or working on hands-on crafts—I love anything that lets me create and explore at the same time. I’ve gained experience as a graphic design intern and through part-time work with a nonprofit, where I learned how to design with purpose and adaptability. I’m especially drawn to rebranding and brand evolution—refreshing visuals while keeping what makes a brand feel like itself.

Collection of Works

A collection of work I’ve developed over the years—from early studio projects in my college foundations to more recent freelance and side gigs.

Studio 1

This is one of the few pieces I created during my first year in foundations studio, where I focused on drawing the human figure and studying anatomy by learning to observe what was directly in front of me. Using charcoal, vine, and black and white acrylics, I explored contrast and how it adds depth and life to a composition. Working from live models, this piece reflects the importance of building strong artistic foundations, especially in understanding form, light, and the structure of the body.

Digital Media

This piece is part of my very early exploration of digital media, where I began learning Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. As a beginner, I focused on understanding the basics of these tools and experimenting with how to create simple, intentional, and visually engaging designs.

Internship Work Different Gigs

These pieces are from my first internship, which later inspired me to pursue a minor in health administration and explore design within the healthcare field. It was my first experience learning Adobe InDesign and understanding how to work with real agencies and organizations. This collection also includes various projects and designs I created for a band and a nonprofit.
